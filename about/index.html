About

Hello, I'm UmHafsa 👋

I'm a mother, a computer science engineer, and someone who is finding her way back to the world of technology after taking time to embrace motherhood.

My Story

Life took a beautiful detour when I became a mom. I stepped away from screens and algorithms to be fully present for the tiny human who needed me most. Now, as she grows, I'm rediscovering my passion for tech — one line of code at a time.

What I Write About

Tech — My journey back into programming, projects I'm building, things I'm learning. From Python fundamentals to web development, I document my path openly and honestly.

Life — Reflections on motherhood, intentional living, and the quiet spaces that exist when you step away from social media. Real thoughts, unfiltered.

Why a Personal Website?

In a world of infinite scroll and attention-grabbing algorithms, I wanted something different. This space is intentional — no ads, no tracking, no notifications. Just words, shared at my own pace.

Get in Touch

If something I wrote resonated with you, I'd love to hear about it. You can find me through this space — just keep reading, and perhaps our paths will cross.

This space is built with Nuxt.js and written in both English and Arabic, because language is home too.
